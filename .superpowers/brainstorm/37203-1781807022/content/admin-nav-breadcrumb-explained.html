How the breadcrumb + anchor bar work

Two distinct strips — one for navigation up the hierarchy, one for jumping within a page.

Strip 1 — Breadcrumb (back-navigation)
Victory Velocity ADMIN
Clients Sign Out
CLIENTS / CHILDSPOT / REPORTS (current page — not a link)
CLIENTS and CHILDSPOT are clickable links. REPORTS is just a label — it tells you where you are.
Clickable items: CLIENTS → takes you back to /admin.   CHILDSPOT → takes you back to /admin/clients/[id].
Current item: The last crumb (REPORTS) is just a label — not a link. It shows where you are.
Strip 2 — Anchor bar (in-page jump, client detail only)
Victory Velocity ADMIN
Clients Sign Out
CLIENTS / CHILDSPOT
CONFIG
USERS
RUNS
REPORTS
All four items are clickable — they scroll you to that section on the same page. RUNS is highlighted because it's in view.
All four clickable: CONFIG, USERS, RUNS, REPORTS are anchor links — clicking one smooth-scrolls you to that section below.
Active highlight: Whichever section is currently in the viewport gets the white underline. Updates as you scroll.
Only on client detail: This bar only appears on /admin/clients/[id] — not on the clients list or report editor.
